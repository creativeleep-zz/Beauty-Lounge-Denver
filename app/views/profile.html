Kasey Thall

Licensed Esthetician

Heather Baker

Licensed Esthetician and Makeup Artist

Heather Baker is a professional makeup artist and licensed esthetician. She loves having the ability to make people feel confident and beautiful with just a few strokes of a brush. She believes every woman is beautiful without makeup but she loves the power of transformation it can have.

Heather went to cosmetology school here in Colorado and makeup school in Los Angeles. She has worked for MAC Cosmetics along with many other prestigious cosmetic lines in the industry and was also a personal shopper for makeup at Nordstrom. Working for these different companies in the beauty industry was such an awesome experience but after a while she decided to start her own company doing makeup professionally and have fallen even more in love with this industry since! Heather has had some awesome opportunities within the industry and have enjoyed doing makeup for TV and print work, music videos, fashion shows, countless photo shoots, weddings and pretty much anything and everything you could need makeup for! One of her biggest accomplishments so far would be traveling to Nashville to work on a movie that was just released. Heather has also had the pleasure of teaching makeup to cosmetology students every month which has been a blessing to share her passion with others. She loves being in this industry and exploring all the different areas and opportunities it has to grow in.

Dawn

Licensed Esthetician

Dawn is a licensed Esthetician in Colorado & in Pennsylvania and a certified lash stylist in both the classic & volume techniques.
